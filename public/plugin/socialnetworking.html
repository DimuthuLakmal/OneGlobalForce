Basically poverty is the condition where a person’s basic needs for food clothing and shelter are not being met. It’s generally two types: i) Absolute poverty synonymous with destitution. It means about the same everywhere and can be eradicated. ii) Relative poverty occurs when people don’t enjoy a certain minimum level of living standards as determined by the government that vary from country to country. This occurs everywhere, is said to be increasing and may not be eradicated. According to my point of view reduction of poverty is not completely lay on the hands of the government of a certain country or international organizations but the people themselves should be able to come out of this disastrous state. I think inside every person there’s a revolutionist and most of us do not want to wake him up just because we fear to or of indolence inside us. If we have a glance at the world, most of the rich people have begun with nothing but with will power and self confidence. As alleviation most of the third world country citizens reach education and health facilities free of charge yet poverty is a major problem. That’s due to the fault in the system of education and regime. Considering these facts having proper education system which increases the literacy of country, development of infrastructural facilities and a proper ruling system are the drive forces to eliminate poverty. In brief education is the process of giving or receiving systematic information together with proper attitudes, life skills and strong personality. Furthermore citizens are responsible for selecting rulers in democratic countries via elections and if the rulers seem to be doing wrong people can change the governing body. So I think poverty reduction in a certain country is a cooperative project involving citizens, the Government, national and international organizations but the initial beginning exists inside us.
